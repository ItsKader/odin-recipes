Protein icecream

Description

Homemade protein icecream that is easy to make and nutritious

Ingredients

  • 1 x medium Bananas, raw
  • 1 x tablespoon Peanut butter, smooth, reduced fat
  • 0.5 x cup Milk, reduced fat, fluid, 2% milkfat, without added vitamin
  • 2 x scoop Gold Standard Chocolate Whey
  • 1 x peanut Peanuts, all types, dry-roasted, with salt
  • 1 x tsp Sweetener, syrup, agave

Steps

  1. Peel and cut banana roughly.
    2. Blend the banana along with peanut butter, whey, milk and sweetener until smooth.
  2. Stir in roasted peanuts in the mixture.
  3. Keep in the freezer for 6 hours or until set.
  4. Serve with chocolate glaze or fruits.
  5. Blend the banana along with peanut butter, milk and sweetener until smooth.
